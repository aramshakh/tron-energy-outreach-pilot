## Design Context

### Users
- Primary user: Roman, the internal champion who must quickly understand the offer and defend it to management.
- Secondary users: TRON Energy management reviewing whether this is a credible, low-risk pilot.
- Job to be done: understand the offer in under a minute, see that this is a controlled outbound pilot rather than a vague technical experiment, and feel safe approving one of the launch packages.

### Brand Personality
- Empy should feel: calm, premium, intelligent.
- Emotional goals: confidence, clarity, control.
- Tone: founder-grade, product-minded, minimal hype, high-trust.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Use Empy-style interface language: refined dark surfaces, soft luminous accents, premium spacing, product-like polish, and a composed, strategic feel rather than generic startup gradients.
- The page should feel like a product system / operating surface, not a generic presentation theme.
- Anti-reference: AI-slop deck design, loud sales gradients, glassmorphism overload, generic SaaS landing page cards.

### Design Principles
1. Reduce buyer risk visually: make the offer feel controlled, structured, and defensible.
2. One screen, one decision: each section should communicate one idea fast.
3. Product, not pitch deck: use interface rhythm, signal rails, and system polish instead of presentation clichés.
4. Premium restraint: minimal hype, high hierarchy, clean spacing, and strong typography.
5. Management-friendly clarity: a warm internal champion should be able to forward this link upward without extra explanation.
