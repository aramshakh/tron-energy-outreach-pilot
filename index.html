03 · problem

Why a database is not a channel

This is the trust slide: name the operational gap without sounding dramatic.
A single explanation Roman can use when “just buy contacts” comes up.

Contacts alone do not turn into pipeline.

A working outbound motion depends on several coordinated layers. Missing one of them usually turns the project into a slow internal experiment.

What a working motion requires
  • Relevant ICP targeting
  • Personal decision-maker emails
  • Segmentation by angle and market role
  • Message sequence design
What usually breaks
  • Good data with no execution layer
  • Execution with poor targeting
  • In-house setup drag
  • No clean hand-off of warm signals
Key point — the expensive part is not access to contacts. It is launching a controlled outbound process that management can trust.
